About Meadowbrook

A Community Built on Compassion

Founded in 1999, Meadowbrook Retirement Home has been a cornerstone of our community — offering personalised, compassionate care in a warm, welcoming environment that feels like home.

💛

Person-Centred Care

Every resident is unique. We tailor care plans to honour each person's history, preferences, and needs.

🤝

Family Partnership

We keep families connected and informed every step of the way — because you're part of our team.

🌿

Dignity & Respect

We uphold the dignity of every resident, fostering independence and a sense of purpose each day.

What We Offer

Care That Grows With You

From independent living to specialised memory care, our full range of services is designed to meet you wherever you are.

🏠

Residential Living

Comfortable, private suites with personalised décor options, housekeeping, and all-inclusive meals in our sunny dining room.

💊

Medical & Nursing Care

24/7 registered nurses on site, medication management, and co-ordination with specialist doctors for peace of mind.

🧠

Memory Care

A safe, specially designed wing for residents living with dementia, staffed by certified memory care specialists.

🧘

Physiotherapy & Wellness

On-site physio, gentle exercise classes, hydrotherapy pool access, and ongoing wellness assessments.

🍽️

Nutritional Dining

Chef-prepared meals daily with dietary accommodations, plus a café lounge open all day for snacks and socialising.

🚐

Transport & Outings

Regular community outings, medical transport, and shuttle services to keep residents connected with the wider world.
