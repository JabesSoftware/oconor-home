Current Project Highlights

We’re building a new 24-bed hospital wing so Buller’s elders can keep receiving hospital- level and dementia care close to home. Plans are approved, the design is complete, groundworks are under way — now we’re asking our community to help close the funding gap. • Total project cost: Approximately $13.4 million • O’Conor Home as at April 2026 $856k invested preliminary groundworks • Funding secured: Trust reserves, bank finance, and Health NZ sustainability funding, private individual donation. • Shortfall to close: Approximately $2.9 million – proposed solution: ◦ $800,000+ community fundraising • Govt/Grants/Corporate/Philanthropic $2,122,472
